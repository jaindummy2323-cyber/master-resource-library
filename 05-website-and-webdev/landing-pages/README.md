## Selection Criteria Applied
- Every repo has significant community adoption
- All actively maintained (commits within 2025-2026)
- Production-ready templates or functional builders
- Responsive, modern design patterns
- Permissive licenses (MIT, Apache 2.0)

## How to Choose
- **SaaS landing page (Next.js)?** -> Taxonomy (shadcn + full stack) or Vercel SaaS Starter
- **Marketing site?** -> Astro Landing Page (fastest) or Cruip templates (beautiful)
- **Drag-and-drop page builder?** -> GrapesJS (most popular) or Puck (React-native)
- **Visual editor for React?** -> Plasmic or Builder.io (use your own components)
- **Email templates?** -> React Email (React components) or MJML (responsive framework)
- **Email with Tailwind?** -> Maizzle (Tailwind + email build system)
